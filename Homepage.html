Welcome to VSU Open Chat AI

In collaboration with my partners, we undertook the design and implementation of the VSU Open AI Chatbot, a sophisticated tool aimed at enhancing the course review experience for students. The chatbot is seamlessly integrated into the VSU system, allowing students to select a specific class and engage in a conversational exchange with the bot.The functionality of the chatbot extends beyond traditional course review platforms. Upon selecting a course, the bot prompts users to share their experiences and feedback about the selected class. It engages users in a dynamic conversation, providing a ersonalized and interactive experience. This approach not only facilitates data collection but also fosters a user-friendly environment for students to express their thoughts and sentiments about their academic journey. To ensure a comprehensive understanding of the students' perspectives, the chatbot stores and logs the conversations. The Chat Log page serves as a centralized repository where the university administration can access and review the data entered by students. This log becomes a valuable resource for the university, offering insights into the overall student sentiment, concerns, and satisfaction levels across various courses.By leveraging this innovative solution, the university gains a real-time, qualitative understanding of how students feel in their classes. This invaluable feedback can inform decision-making processes, curriculum enhancements, and faculty support initiatives. Ultimately, the VSU Open AI Chatbot empowers both students and the university administration, fostering a collaborative and responsive educational environment.
